# App Store Prep

## Proposed App Name

Venkata Task Hub

## Subtitle Ideas

- Mortgage and Investing Tools
- Personal Finance Calculators
- Finance Tools for Planning

## App Store Description Draft

Venkata Task Hub is a lightweight personal finance utility app focused on fast, repeatable calculations.

The first release includes:

- a mortgage amortization calculator with payoff schedule and total-cost visibility
- a compound interest calculator with lower, base, and higher growth scenarios
- saved scenarios for quick reuse
- native iPhone charts and offline calculation support

This app is designed for practical planning rather than account aggregation or trading. It keeps the experience simple, fast, and private.

## Keywords Draft

mortgage, amortization, compound interest, investment, calculator, finance, loan, payoff, savings

## Privacy Position

- No account required
- No third-party analytics
- No advertising
- Saved scenarios are stored on-device only

## Assets Still Needed

## Xcode / App Store Connect Checklist

1. Open the project in full Xcode.
2. Set your Apple Developer team and confirm signing.
3. Replace the placeholder app icon.
4. Build and test on at least one iPhone simulator and one real device if possible.
5. Create the app in App Store Connect.
6. Fill in description, keywords, support URL, and privacy answers.
7. Upload screenshots.
8. Archive and upload the build from Xcode.
9. Submit for App Review.
